Keto Chicken Salad

Description

Grilled chicken salad is a healthy eater favorite for a reason:
Lean protein plus lots of vegetables equals a simple, nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 avocado sliced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic salt

Steps

  1. Drizzle 2 tbs olive oil in pan, cook chicken breasts until internal temperature reaches 165F, then cut into pieces
  2. In bowl, mix spinach, cheese, sunflower seeds, and avocado
  3. In small container mix remainder of olive oil, salt, and garlic salt
  4. Let dressing sit for 5 minutes and than apply dressing and mix into salad
  5. Finito!
